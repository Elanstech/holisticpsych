FAQs

Common Questions

How do I schedule my first appointment?

Call us at (203) 823-1015 or fill out the contact form above. We'll respond within 24 hours to schedule your consultation.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Contact us to verify your specific coverage.

Do you offer telehealth sessions?

Yes, we offer secure telehealth appointments for your convenience. Choose in-person or virtual sessions based on your preference.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Bring your insurance card, a valid ID, and any relevant medical records. Arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete paperwork.

How long are therapy sessions?

Standard sessions are 45-50 minutes. Initial consultations may be longer to allow for comprehensive assessment.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.
