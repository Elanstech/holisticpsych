Have Questions?

Find answers to common questions about scheduling, insurance, and our services.

You can schedule your first appointment by filling out the contact form above, calling us directly at (123) 456-7890, or emailing info@holisticpsych.com. We'll respond within 24 hours to confirm your appointment time.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment options for those without insurance coverage. Contact us to verify your specific plan and discuss payment arrangements.

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical records or previous assessments. We'll send you intake forms to complete before your first visit.

Yes! We offer secure telehealth sessions for clients who prefer virtual appointments or cannot visit our office in person. Our telehealth platform is HIPAA-compliant and easy to use.

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.

Standard sessions are 50 minutes. Initial consultations may be longer (60-90 minutes) to allow time for comprehensive assessment. We also offer extended sessions if needed.
