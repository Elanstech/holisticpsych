Meet Laura

Founder & Lead Aesthetician

For over 15 years, I've dedicated my life to the art of natural beauty and holistic wellness. What started as a passion for helping people feel confident in their own skin has grown into a sanctuary where every treatment is a personalized journey to radiance.

My philosophy is simple: true beauty comes from treating the whole person. That's why every facial, every waxing session, every service at Laura's Beauty Touch is customized to your unique needs, skin type, and wellness goals.

I believe in the power of organic, sustainable products that work with your skin, not against it. Every product I use is carefully selected for its purity and effectiveness, because you deserve nothing less than the best nature has to offer.

Our Philosophy

At Laura's Beauty Touch, we believe that wellness is a journey, not a destination. Every person who walks through our doors deserves to feel seen, heard, and valued. We take the time to understand your skin, your concerns, and your goals — because when you feel good in your skin, you radiate confidence from the inside out.

We're not just about surface-level beauty. We're about creating a holistic experience that nurtures your body, calms your mind, and lifts your spirit. That's the Laura's Beauty Touch difference.
