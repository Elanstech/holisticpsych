About Us

Dedicated to Your Mental Wellness

At Holistic Psychological Services, we believe in treating the whole person. Our team of experienced psychologists provides compassionate, evidence-based care in a welcoming Manhattan office.

Founded by Dr. Kristie Doheny and Dr. Douglas Uhlig, our practice specializes in creating personalized treatment plans that address your unique needs, combining traditional therapeutic approaches with innovative techniques to help you achieve lasting wellness.

A Welcoming Space for Everyone

We proudly serve individuals and families of all backgrounds, identities, and orientations. Our practice is LGBTQ+ affirming, and we celebrate diversity in all its forms. Whether you're seeking support for yourself, your relationship, or your family, you'll find a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment here.
