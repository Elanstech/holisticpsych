Depression Treatment

Compassionate, Evidence-Based Care for Lasting Relief

Depression is more than just feeling sadâ€”it's a complex mental health condition that affects how you think, feel, and function in daily life. Our comprehensive depression treatment program combines evidence-based therapies with personalized care to help you reclaim your joy and vitality.

Whether you're experiencing persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, changes in sleep or appetite, or difficulty concentrating, our experienced psychologists are here to help. We understand that every person's experience with depression is unique, and we tailor our approach to meet your specific needs.

What We Treat

Major Depressive Disorder
Persistent Depressive Disorder
Seasonal Affective Disorder
Postpartum Depression
Treatment-Resistant Depression
Depression with Anxiety

Our Treatment Process

01

Comprehensive Assessment

We begin with a thorough evaluation to understand your symptoms, medical history, and life circumstances. This includes standardized assessments and in-depth clinical interviews.

02

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on your assessment, we create a customized treatment plan that may include Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Interpersonal Therapy, mindfulness techniques, or a combination of approaches.

03

Regular Therapy Sessions

Through weekly or bi-weekly sessions, we work together to challenge negative thought patterns, develop coping strategies, and build resilience. We track your progress and adjust treatment as needed.

04

Ongoing Support & Relapse Prevention

As you progress, we focus on long-term wellness, teaching you tools to maintain your mental health and prevent future episodes. We're here for you even after active treatment concludes.

Anxiety Disorders Treatment

Find Peace and Control Over Worry and Fear

Anxiety can be overwhelming, manifesting as persistent worry, panic attacks, or avoidance behaviors that limit your life. Our anxiety treatment program helps you understand the root causes of your anxiety and develop practical skills to manage symptoms effectively.

Using proven therapeutic approaches, we help you break free from the cycle of anxious thoughts and behaviors. Our goal is to empower you with tools and strategies that provide lasting relief and improve your quality of life.

Conditions We Treat

Generalized Anxiety Disorder
Panic Disorder
Social Anxiety
Specific Phobias
Health Anxiety
Performance Anxiety

Our Treatment Approach

01

Anxiety Assessment

We conduct a detailed evaluation to identify your specific anxiety triggers, physical symptoms, and thought patterns. Understanding your unique anxiety profile is key to effective treatment.

02

Cognitive Restructuring

Learn to identify and challenge anxious thoughts. We teach you techniques to reframe negative thinking patterns and develop more balanced perspectives.

03

Exposure Therapy

Gradually and safely face feared situations in a controlled environment. This evidence-based approach helps reduce avoidance behaviors and build confidence.

04

Relaxation & Coping Skills

Master practical techniques including breathing exercises, progressive muscle relaxation, and mindfulness to manage anxiety in real-time situations.

PTSD & Trauma Recovery

Healing from Past Wounds with Specialized Care

Trauma can leave lasting impacts on your emotional, physical, and mental well-being. Whether you've experienced a single traumatic event or ongoing trauma, our specialized trauma therapy provides a safe, supportive environment for healing and recovery.

Our trauma-informed approach recognizes the profound effects of traumatic experiences and focuses on building safety, trust, and empowerment. We use evidence-based treatments specifically designed for trauma recovery, helping you process difficult memories and reclaim your life.

Trauma Types We Address

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder
Complex Trauma
Childhood Trauma
Domestic Violence Trauma
Accident/Injury Trauma
Acute Stress Disorder

Trauma Healing Journey

01

Safety & Stabilization

We prioritize creating a safe therapeutic relationship and teaching grounding techniques. You learn to manage symptoms and develop coping strategies before processing traumatic memories.

02

Trauma Processing

Using evidence-based methods like CPT (Cognitive Processing Therapy) or PE (Prolonged Exposure), we help you safely process traumatic memories and reduce their emotional intensity.

03

Integration & Growth

As trauma symptoms diminish, we focus on rebuilding your sense of self, improving relationships, and finding meaning. You develop a new narrative that incorporates your experiences without being defined by them.

04

Long-Term Resilience

We equip you with ongoing coping strategies and help you recognize triggers. Our goal is sustainable recovery and post-traumatic growth, enabling you to thrive beyond survival.

Behavioral Health Services

Transforming Behaviors, Enhancing Well-Being

Behavioral health encompasses the connection between behaviors and overall mental and physical well-being. Our behavioral health services address a wide range of concerns including stress management, habit formation, anger management, and lifestyle changes that impact mental health.

We take a holistic approach to behavioral health, recognizing that our daily actions, routines, and choices significantly affect our mental wellness. Through evidence-based behavioral interventions, we help you develop healthier patterns and achieve lasting change.

Areas of Focus

Stress Management
Anger Management
Sleep Disorders
Substance Use Concerns
Eating Behaviors
Life Transitions

Behavioral Change Process

01

Behavioral Analysis

We identify patterns, triggers, and consequences of current behaviors. This functional analysis helps us understand what maintains problematic behaviors and what changes are needed.

02

Goal Setting & Planning

Together, we establish clear, achievable goals and create a structured plan for change. We break down large goals into manageable steps and identify potential obstacles.

03

Skill Building & Practice

Learn and practice new coping skills, stress management techniques, and healthier behavioral alternatives. We use role-playing, homework assignments, and real-world practice.

04

Maintenance & Relapse Prevention

Develop strategies to maintain positive changes and manage setbacks. We focus on building long-term habits that support your mental health and overall well-being.

ADHD Assessment & Treatment

Comprehensive Evaluation and Support for Focus Challenges

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) affects both children and adults, impacting focus, organization, impulse control, and daily functioning. Our comprehensive ADHD services include thorough diagnostic assessments and evidence-based treatments tailored to your age and specific needs.

Whether you're seeking an initial diagnosis or support managing known ADHD, our experienced psychologists provide detailed evaluations and practical strategies to help you succeed at work, school, and in relationships.

Our ADHD Services

Comprehensive Testing
Adult ADHD Evaluation
Child ADHD Assessment
Executive Function Testing
Treatment Planning
Coaching & Skills Training

Assessment & Treatment Process

01

Initial Consultation

We discuss your concerns, symptoms, and history. This includes gathering information about challenges at work, school, or home, and understanding how symptoms impact daily life.

02

Comprehensive Testing

Our thorough assessment includes standardized testing, behavioral observations, symptom rating scales, and often input from family members or teachers. We evaluate attention, impulsivity, executive functioning, and rule out other conditions.

03

Diagnosis & Feedback

You receive a detailed report explaining test results, diagnosis (if applicable), and specific recommendations. We ensure you fully understand the findings and what they mean for treatment.

04

Treatment & Support

Based on assessment results, we provide cognitive-behavioral therapy, organizational skills training, time management strategies, and can coordinate with prescribers if medication is appropriate.

Family Support & Therapy

Strengthening Bonds, Resolving Conflicts Together

Families face unique challenges that can strain relationships and affect the well-being of all members. Our family therapy services provide a safe, neutral space where families can address conflicts, improve communication, and strengthen their connections.

We recognize that family dynamics are complex and each family member's perspective matters. Our systemic approach considers how individual behaviors affect the whole family system, helping everyone work together toward positive change and healthier relationships.

Family Issues We Address

Parent-Child Conflict
Divorce & Blended Families
Sibling Relationships
Behavioral Issues
Grief & Loss
Major Life Transitions

Family Therapy Process

01

Family Assessment

We meet with the family to understand the challenges, family structure, communication patterns, and each member's perspective. This helps us identify areas of strength and concern.

02

Goal Collaboration

Together, the family establishes shared goals for therapy. We ensure everyone's voice is heard and work toward objectives that benefit the entire family system.

03

Communication & Conflict Resolution

We teach effective communication skills, active listening, and healthy conflict resolution strategies. Family members practice these skills in session and at home.

04

Strengthening Relationships

As communication improves, we focus on rebuilding trust, enhancing emotional connections, and creating sustainable patterns that support long-term family harmony.

Couples Counseling

Rebuilding Connection, Communication & Intimacy

Every relationship faces challenges. Whether you're dealing with communication breakdowns, trust issues, intimacy concerns, or simply feeling disconnected, couples counseling provides a supportive environment to work through difficulties and strengthen your bond.

Our couples therapists help partners understand each other's perspectives, identify unhealthy patterns, and develop skills for healthier communication and conflict resolution. We work with couples at all stagesâ€”from those considering marriage to those navigating long-term relationships.

Common Relationship Concerns

Trust & Infidelity Recovery

Conflict & Anger

Life Transition Support

Couples Therapy Journey

01

Relationship Assessment

We meet with both partners to understand your relationship history, current challenges, and individual perspectives. We identify patterns of interaction and areas needing attention.

02

Creating Safety & Understanding

We establish ground rules for respectful dialogue and help each partner feel heard and understood. Learning to communicate without defensiveness or blame is foundational.

03

Addressing Core Issues

We work through specific challenges using evidence-based approaches like Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) or the Gottman Method, helping you understand emotional needs and attachment patterns.

04

Rebuilding & Moving Forward

As you develop healthier communication and conflict skills, we focus on rebuilding trust, intimacy, and connection. You learn tools to maintain relationship health long after therapy ends.

Child Psychology Services

Supporting Children's Emotional Growth & Development

Children face unique emotional and behavioral challenges as they grow and develop. Our child psychology services provide specialized assessment and treatment for children ages 5-17, helping them navigate difficulties and develop healthy coping skills.

We use developmentally appropriate, evidence-based approaches that make therapy engaging and effective for children. Our child psychologists work closely with parents, offering guidance and strategies to support their child's mental health at home and school.

Childhood Issues We Address

Anxiety & Fears
Behavioral Problems
Social Skills Difficulties
School-Related Stress
Emotional Regulation
Trauma & Adjustment Issues

Child Therapy Process

01

Parent Consultation

We begin by meeting with parents to understand concerns, developmental history, and current challenges. This helps us gain important context before meeting your child.

02

Child Assessment

Through play, conversation, and age-appropriate activities, we assess your child's emotional state, behaviors, and needs. We create a safe, comfortable environment where children feel free to express themselves.

03

Play & Talk Therapy

Using play therapy, art, games, and talk therapy (depending on age), we help children process emotions, develop coping skills, and practice new behaviors in a supportive setting.

04

Parent Collaboration

Regular parent sessions ensure you understand your child's progress and learn strategies to support them at home. We provide guidance on behavior management, communication, and emotional support.
