Our Process

How Treatment Works With Us

We've designed a straightforward, supportive process to help you start your healing journey with confidence.

01

Initial Contact

Reach out via phone, email, or our online form. We'll respond within 24 hours to schedule your consultation and answer any questions.
02

Comprehensive Assessment

Your first session is a thorough evaluation where we learn about your concerns, history, goals, and what you hope to achieve through therapy.
03

Personalized Treatment Plan

We collaborate with you to create a customized treatment plan that addresses your specific needs using evidence-based approaches.
04

Ongoing Therapy Sessions

Regular sessions (typically weekly or bi-weekly) where we work together using proven techniques to help you reach your goals.
05

Progress Monitoring

We regularly assess your progress and adjust treatment as needed to ensure you're moving toward your wellness goals.
06

Achieving Your Goals

As you reach your treatment goals, we develop a maintenance plan and prepare for successful completion or reduced frequency of sessions.
Treatment Methods

Evidence-Based Therapy Approaches

We utilize proven therapeutic techniques backed by research and tailored to your unique needs.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors to improve emotional well-being.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Family Systems Therapy

FAQ

Common Questions About Our Services

How long does therapy typically last?

The duration of therapy varies based on individual needs and goals. Some clients benefit from short-term focused therapy (8-12 sessions), while others prefer longer-term support. We work with you to determine what's best for your situation.

Do you offer telehealth sessions?

Yes! We offer secure telehealth sessions via video conferencing for clients who prefer virtual appointments or cannot come to our Manhattan office.

How do I know which therapist is right for me?

During your initial consultation, we'll discuss your concerns and match you with a therapist whose expertise and approach best fits your needs. The therapeutic relationship is crucial, and we want to ensure you feel comfortable.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is an assessment where we learn about your background, current concerns, and goals. We'll discuss confidentiality, answer your questions, and begin developing your personalized treatment plan.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes, absolutely. We maintain strict HIPAA compliance and confidentiality. Information is only shared with your explicit written consent, except in rare cases required by law (such as imminent danger to self or others).

Can I switch therapists if needed?

Of course! Finding the right therapeutic fit is essential. If you feel another therapist might be a better match, we'll work with you to facilitate a smooth transition within our practice.
