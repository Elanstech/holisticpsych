Our Journey

A Decade of Making a Difference

2010

The Beginning

Dr. Kristie Doheny and Dr. Douglas Uhlig founded Holistic Psychological Services with a vision to provide comprehensive, compassionate mental health care in Manhattan.

2013

Expanding Our Team

As demand for our services grew, we expanded our team to include specialized therapists in child psychology, couples counseling, and trauma recovery.

2017

New Madison Ave Location

Moved to our beautiful new office space at 274 Madison Avenue, providing clients with a modern, welcoming environment designed for healing.

2020

Telehealth Innovation

Pioneered comprehensive telehealth services, ensuring uninterrupted care during the pandemic and expanding access to mental health support.

2023

500+ Lives Transformed

Reached a major milestone by helping over 500 individuals and families achieve lasting mental wellness and life transformation.

2025

Looking Forward

Continuing to innovate and expand our services while maintaining the personalized, compassionate care that defines our practice.
