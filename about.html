Our Journey

A Practice Built on Passion & Purpose

2010

The Beginning

Holistic Psychological Services was founded by Dr. Kristie Doheny and Dr. Douglas Uhlig with a shared vision: to create a psychology practice that truly puts clients first. Both seasoned professionals with extensive backgrounds in clinical psychology, they recognized a need for more personalized, holistic mental health care in Manhattan.

2015

Expanding Our Team

As word spread about our compassionate approach and proven results, we began expanding our team with carefully selected clinicians who shared our values. Each new team member was chosen not just for their credentials, but for their dedication to client-centered care and their ability to create genuine connections.

Today

A Thriving Practice

Now with six expert clinicians, we serve hundreds of individuals, couples, and families each year. Our practice has become known throughout Manhattan for its warm atmosphere, innovative treatment approaches, and genuine commitment to helping people live their best lives. We're proud to be LGBTQ+ affirming and to welcome clients from all walks of life.
