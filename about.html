Our Mission

Holistic Healing for Mind & Spirit

At Holistic Psychological Services, we believe in treating the whole person—not just symptoms. Our approach combines evidence-based therapeutic techniques with deep compassion and understanding.

Founded by Dr. Kristie Doheny and Dr. Douglas Uhlig, our practice is built on the principle that everyone deserves access to exceptional mental health care in a safe, welcoming environment.

We specialize in creating personalized treatment plans that honor your unique experiences, challenges, and goals. Whether you're seeking individual therapy, couples counseling, or family support, we're here to guide you on your journey to wellness.

Our Core Values

What Guides Our Approach to Care

Compassionate Care

We approach every client with empathy, understanding that healing begins with being truly heard and accepted.

Evidence-Based Methods

Our treatments are grounded in proven therapeutic approaches backed by the latest psychological research.

Inclusive Environment

We welcome individuals and families of all backgrounds, identities, and orientations in a judgment-free space.

Personalized Treatment

Every treatment plan is uniquely tailored to your specific needs, goals, and life circumstances.

Complete Confidentiality
