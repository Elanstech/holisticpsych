Dr. Kristie Doheny

PsyD LMSW LMHC RPT-S Licensed Clinical Psychologist

About Dr. Doheny

While we can't change difficult situations of the past, we can work together to better understand and resolve challenges in your life. By applying complementary therapy approaches and techniques, we will unearth long-standing behavior patterns or negative perceptions that may be holding you back from experiencing a more fulfilling and meaningful life.

If you're looking for extra support and guidance through a challenging situation or you're just ready to move in a new direction in your life, I look forward to working with you to achieve your goals.

Experience & Specialization

With over 10 years of experience working with children, adolescents, and families, I specialize in person-centered therapy which enables me to create individualized treatment plans designed specifically to help families and young people reach their personal goals.

Dr. Douglas J. Uhlig

PhD MBA CASAC Licensed Clinical Psychologist

Philosophy & Approach

Within each of us is a deeper or higher self we can contact. Through Meditation, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Hypnosis, and Gestalt Therapy, I help people contact this part of themselves, which is always available to help in times of need.

I am a psychologist and therapist who focuses on working with adults in areas including marriage counseling, ADHD, alcohol and substance abuse, identity formation and parenting issues. I address feelings of emptiness, self-esteem, depression, anxiety, fear, and anger.

Professional Background

I am a Licensed Psychologist (NYS 018462) who combines traditional Psychodynamic and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy with over 35 years of teaching meditation and Mindfulness. What distinguishes my approach is my emphasis on contacting the wiser and deeper part of oneself.

Tiffany Aguilar

Pre-Licensed Professional Mental Health Counselor in Training

About Tiffany

You don't need to have everything figured out to start therapy. You may be seeking support for anxiety that can't seem to quiet down or depression that has been making it hard to get out of bed. Maybe you're feeling overwhelmed dealing with the effects of past or present trauma. You may be worried for a loved one struggling with emotional regulation, attachment, or identity development issues. No matter the issue, therapy can provide a supportive, safe space to understand your emotions and develop effective coping skills.

I am a mental health counselor in training with 7+ years of experience working with children and adolescents. With my background in implementing Applied Behavioral Analysis, I aim to combine evidence-based interventions with skill-building strategies to help improve emotional regulation, productive communication, and daily functioning.

Starting therapy and finding the right therapist can be nerve-racking, but it is the first step of the therapeutic process. I strive to create a collaborative, supportive environment where my clients feel understood. If you feel like I may be a good fit for you, I would be happy to discuss further!

About Makeda

Seeking support is a courageous step, and I would be honored to be part of your journey toward greater self-awareness and emotional wellness. I am a compassionate and motivated Mental Health Counselor with a strong background in healthcare, client support, and crisis response. I have exceptional interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to provide you with detailed attention and empathy. I am also dedicated to supporting diverse populations through active listening, cultural awareness, and evidence-based mental health practices.
