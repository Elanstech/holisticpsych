Dr. Kristie Doheny

PsyD LMSW LMHC RPT-S Licensed Clinical Psychologist

About Dr. Doheny

While we can't change difficult situations of the past, we can work together to better understand and resolve challenges in your life. By applying complementary therapy approaches and techniques, we will unearth long-standing behavior patterns or negative perceptions that may be holding you back from experiencing a more fulfilling and meaningful life.

If you're looking for extra support and guidance through a challenging situation or you're just ready to move in a new direction in your life, I look forward to working with you to achieve your goals.

Experience & Specialization

With over 10 years of experience working with children, adolescents, and families, I specialize in person-centered therapy which enables me to create individualized treatment plans designed specifically to help families and young people reach their personal goals.

Treatment Approaches

Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy
Evidence-based CBT techniques to address thought patterns and behavioral challenges in children and families
Neurofeedback & Biofeedback
Advanced techniques for brain training and physiological regulation in children with ADHD
Family-Centered Therapy
Holistic approach tailored to each family's unique needs and goals

Philosophy & Approach

Within each of us is a deeper or higher self we can contact. Through Meditation, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Hypnosis, and Gestalt Therapy, I help people contact this part of themselves, which is always available to help in times of need.

I am a psychologist and therapist who focuses on working with adults in areas including marriage counseling, ADHD, alcohol and substance abuse, identity formation and parenting issues. I address feelings of emptiness, self-esteem, depression, anxiety, fear, and anger.

Professional Background

I am a Licensed Psychologist (NYS 018462) who combines traditional Psychodynamic and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy with over 35 years of teaching meditation and Mindfulness. What distinguishes my approach is my emphasis on contacting the wiser and deeper part of oneself.

Therapeutic Philosophy

When words aren't enough, healing can come through creativity, connection, and care. In therapy, I offer a space where creative expression honors your story and supports your healing.

Viewing each person through a systemic and holistic lens allows me to understand the many layers of identity, experience, and meaning that shape who you are. Together, we can explore the unknown with curiosity and creativity, while understanding not only what happened but also who you are becoming.

My Integrative Approach

My approach is integrative and multidisciplinary, blending talk therapy with expressive arts to explore what words alone cannot capture. Through drawing, collage, movement, and storytelling, our sessions become a space to process what feels unseen or unspoken.

I offer therapy in both English and Turkish, creating an accessible and culturally sensitive environment. The blend of systemic and creative therapy can be a way to discover emotions, find your inner voice and strength, rebuild self-compassion, and accept challenges with courage along the way.
