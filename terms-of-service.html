Professional Services

Nature of Services

We provide professional psychological assessment, psychotherapy, counseling, and related mental health services. Our services are provided by licensed psychologists and mental health professionals in accordance with New York State regulations and professional ethical standards.

Therapeutic Relationship

The therapeutic relationship is a professional relationship designed to support your mental health and well-being. While we strive to provide the highest quality care, we cannot guarantee specific outcomes or results from treatment.

Scope of Practice

Our services do not include emergency or crisis intervention. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please call 911, go to your nearest emergency room, or contact the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 988.

Appointments and Scheduling

Scheduling

Appointments may be scheduled by phone, email, or through our secure patient portal. Initial consultations are typically scheduled for 60 minutes, while follow-up sessions are 50 minutes in length.

Cancellation Policy

We require at least 24 hours advance notice for appointment cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice, or missed appointments without notification, will be charged the full session fee. Exceptions may be made for emergencies or unavoidable circumstances.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive late to your appointment, the session will still end at the scheduled time, and you will be charged for the full session. We recommend arriving 5-10 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

Telehealth Services

We offer telehealth sessions via secure, HIPAA-compliant video conferencing. You are responsible for ensuring a private, quiet location with reliable internet connectivity for your telehealth appointments.

Communication Outside of Sessions

Contact Methods

Between sessions, you may contact us via phone or email for scheduling or brief administrative matters. Email and phone messages are not appropriate for clinical emergencies or lengthy therapeutic discussions.

Response Time

We strive to respond to non-urgent messages within 24-48 business hours. For urgent clinical matters, please call our office. For emergencies, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.

Social Media

To maintain appropriate professional boundaries and protect your privacy, we do not accept friend requests or interact with current or former patients on social media platforms.

Termination of Services

Your Right to Terminate

You have the right to terminate therapy at any time. We recommend discussing your decision with your therapist to ensure a smooth transition and appropriate referrals if needed.

Our Right to Terminate

We may terminate the therapeutic relationship if:

In such cases, we will provide you with appropriate referrals and a summary of your treatment for continuity of care.
